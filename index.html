Welcome to Kaduna

Kaduna State offers a blend of historical landmarks, cultural attractions, and natural beauty.

Top three places to visit in Kaduna

Kajuru Castle

A 1970s European-style fairytale fortress, offering a unique, adventurous, and private getaway for groups or couples.

Matsirga Waterfalls

Breathtaking 30-meter falls fed by "River Wonderful," dropping into a rocky gorge. A tranquil, beautiful spot for views, breezes, and even swimming.

Kamuku National Park

A Nigerian wildlife sanctuary preserving rare plants and diverse fauna. With 1775 bird species, it's excellent for birdwatching.
